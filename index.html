About Orangevale AI Solutions

Founded in 2023, Orangevale AI Solutions was born from a simple observation: businesses were losing money every single day because they couldn't respond to customer inquiries fast enough. Our founder, Dan Downey, spent years in digital marketing and saw firsthand how many leads went cold simply because response times were too slow. We decided to build the solution we wished existed—an intelligent AI system that never sleeps, never gets tired, and always responds with professionalism and personality. Today, we're proud to serve dozens of businesses across Orangevale and beyond, helping them capture more leads, close more deals, and deliver better customer experiences.

Our mission is simple: empower every business, regardless of size, to compete like the big players. We believe that access to advanced AI technology shouldn't be limited to Fortune 500 companies. That's why we've built a platform that's powerful enough for enterprises but affordable and easy to use for small businesses. We're committed to continuous innovation, exceptional customer support, and delivering measurable results that directly impact your bottom line. When you partner with us, you're not just getting a chatbot—you're getting a dedicated partner invested in your success.
